Massive Migration from Syria to Northern European Countries

As war spreads throughout the middle east, many families are displaced, forcing them to seek refuge wherever they can. Due to the fact that the United States is currently refusing refugees entry into our country, refugees have nowhere to go but to northern Europe. What does this mean for Northern European countries? A lot is left up to debate but one thing is for certain: Europe cannot take every single displaced family out there.
